Catching Fruit



Catching fruit involves using collisions and random integers to make fruit fall from different spots along the screen.


We had to learn about how to utilize the arrow keys so the user was involved and we had gravity play a part in the speed of the fruit falling.


Turtle Adventure



Turtle Adventure uses arrow keys to move a turtle throughout the ocean. The turtle tries to collide with different colored bubbles.


The bubbles correspond to different point values. A timer and score are displayed on the screen as well.
